The Problem

Horse matchmaking still runs on word-of-mouth and luck.

  • Owners rely on fragmented Facebook groups and cold calls — missing ideal matches outside their local network.

  • No standard way to vet compatibility by breed, temperament, or bloodline — decisions made on incomplete information.

  • Owner contact details shared upfront with strangers before any mutual interest is established — no privacy layer.

The Solution

A modern matchmaking app built specifically for the equine world.

Swipe through detailed horse profiles filtered by breed, age, location, and temperament. Connect only after a mutual match — keeping owners in full control, always.
